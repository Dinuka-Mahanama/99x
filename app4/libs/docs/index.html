Scandit Data Capture SDK Documentation for Android#

Welcome to the Scandit Data Capture SDK Developer Documentation for Android. If you are trying out the SDK or you are already integrating it into an app, you’ve come to the right place.

If you want to see the SDK in action, take a look at our free Scandit Barcode Scanner Demo app in the Google Play Store.

Subscription & Dashboard Support & Contact

To develop with the Scandit Data Capture SDK, you need an active subscription and a license key to use in your app:

  • Login at scandit.com to create licenses
